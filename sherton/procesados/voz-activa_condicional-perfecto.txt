El Condicional Perfecto -Conditional Perfect o Past Conditional-, es el tiempo que responde a la pregunta: What would have happened? (¿Qué habría pasado?) o What would have been happening? (¿Qué habría estado pasando?).
Las expresiones se estructuran en forma
No Continua
o forma
Continua
• Sujeto + would + have + verbo en participio (I would have worked).
• Sujeto + would + have been + verbo en progresivo (I would have been working).
