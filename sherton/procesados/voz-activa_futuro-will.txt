El tiempo Futuro responde a la pregunta: What will happen? (¿Qué pasará?) o What will be happening? (¿Qué estará pasando?).
Las expresiones se estructuran en forma
No Continua
o forma
Continua
• Sujeto + will +	verbo presente (I will work).
• Sujeto + will + be + gerundio (I will be working).

I am working

3. Pasado

4. Pasado Perfecto

5. Futuro

6. Futuro Perfecto

7. Futuro - going to
I am going to work

I am going to have worked

9. Futuro - en pasado
I was going to work
I was going to be working
10. Futuro Perfecto - en pasado
I was going to have worked
I was going to have been working
11. Condicional
I would work
I would be working
12. Condicional Perfecto
I would have worked
I would have been working
13. Modales
I (can, could, ...) work
I (can, could, ...) be working
14. Modales + have
I (can, could, ...) have worked
I (can, could, ...) have been working
15. Imperativos
Work!
Let's work!
