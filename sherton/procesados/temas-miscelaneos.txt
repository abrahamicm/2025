¡Regístrate!Temas importantes de la gramática inglesa: There be, to ge, to be able to, las contracciones, el gerundio, etc...
There be
- haber (de existir)
there is/are; there was/were; there will be; …
To get
- conseguir, ponerse, volverse, etc.
to get tired, to get sick, to get angry
To be able to
- ser capaz de, poder
I will be able to speak English.
Used to/would
- soler
I used to smoke a lot when I was younger.
To be/get used to
- estar acostumbrado a / acostumbrarse a
I am not used to flying.
To have/get something done
- mandar a hacer algo
I had my house painted.
To want somebody to do something
- quiero que tú hagas
She doesn't want us to call her.
To do/make
- hacer
Do me a favor. Make some tea.
Contracciones
She's left (she's = she has).	She's here (she's = she is)
Expresar Acuerdo -
yo también, yo tampoco
She likes baseball and I do too - She doesn't like rugby and I don't either.
El Gerundio - La forma "ing" del verbo
to run - running; to go - going; to work - working
Verbos seguidos por Gerundios
I enjoy/miss/avoid/detest/… living in New York.
Verbos seguidos por Gerundios o Infinitivos
They started/like/stopped… running/to run.
