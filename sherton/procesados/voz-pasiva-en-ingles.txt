¡Regístrate!Todos los tiempos verbales del inglés en voz pasiva con ejemplos traducidos al español.
Presente -
What is done?
The car is washed
Presente Perfecto -
What has been done?
The car has been washed
Pasado -
What was done?
The car was washed
Pasado Perfecto -
What had been done?
The car had been washed
Futuro -
What will be done?
The car will be washed
Futuro Perfecto -
What will have been done?
The car will have been washed
Futuro (going to) -
What is going to be done?
The car is going to be washed
Futuro Perfecto (going to) -
What is going to have been done?
The car is going to have been washed
Futuro en Pasado -
What was going to be done?
The car was going to be washed
Futuro Perfecto en Pasado -
What was going to have been done?
The car was going to have been washed
Condicional -
What would be done?
The car would be washed
Condicional Perfecto -
What would have been done?
The car would have been washed
Modales -
What (could, must, should, …) be done?
The car (could, must, should,	…) be washed
Modales+Have -
What (could, must, should, …) have been done?
The car (could, must, should, …) have been washed
